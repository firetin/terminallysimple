# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We aim for a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone.

**Expected behavior:**
* Be respectful and kind
* Accept constructive feedback gracefully
* Focus on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
* Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
* Publishing others' private information
* Other unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Report issues to [porttek0@gmail.com](mailto:porttek0@gmail.com). All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
